Our Story

A Salon That
Feels Like Home

Kolors Beauty Salon & Threading was built on a simple belief: every person who walks through the door deserves to feel heard, cared for, and confident when they leave.

Tucked into the heart of Cumming, we've earned a reputation as a true hidden gem — a place where skilled hands, honest prices, and genuine warmth come together. No rushing, no upselling. Just great work, every time.

Proudly women-owned and operated, we believe that's reflected in every service we offer and every relationship we build with the people who trust us with their look.
